About Us!

At Terra Feed, we are passionate about addressing the global issue of food waste through our waste food management application. Our user-friendly web platform connects surplus food with those in need, promoting responsible waste management practices. With real-time notifications and a double verification process, we ensure efficient and secure food redistribution. We go beyond just connecting donors and recipients by providing guidelines for proper disposal of spoiled food and emphasizing sustainable practices. Through analytics and insights, we track our collective impact and continuously improve our platform. Join us in creating a more sustainable future where no edible food goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious meals.
